Olbrich Botanical Gardens!

What are the Olbrich Botanical Gardens?

Located at 3330 Atwood Ave, Madison, Wisconsin, the Olbrich Gardens is a conservatory which will trasnport you to a serene vacation. Just outside of the city, the Gardens consist of a variety of plants and exotic foliage which bloom dependent on the time of year. The pavilion pictured was donated by the government of Thailand.

How to access the gardens

The Olbrich Botanical Garden's are open daily and free to the public. This attraction is one of my most favorite spots in Madison because they serve as an escape from daily stressors, and a place for relaxation and exploration.
